The project in brief

The rules for this page are simple. Every day One drawing, One line of code, and one line of writing. This is also somewhat of a diary, so every day, a recrimination or piece of autobiography (potentially). In doing so, I hope to forever put away the sensation that I am somehow wasting time by not doing art, and also perfect my mastery of html coding (also CSS), all while gushing about my personal inner state and thoughts.
